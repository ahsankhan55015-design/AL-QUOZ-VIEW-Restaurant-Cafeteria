Our Story

Taste of God's Own Country

Nestled in the bustling streets of Al Quoz, we bring the rich, spice-laden traditions of Kerala cuisine straight to your plate. From slow-cooked beef to fragrant ghee rice, every dish is crafted with authentic recipes and a whole lot of heart.
